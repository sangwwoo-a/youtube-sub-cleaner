Key Features

Everything you need for a smarter morning.

🌦️

Weather-Linked Alarm

When rain or snow is forecast, your alarm automatically moves earlier to give you a relaxed morning.

⏰

Custom Lead Time

Set exactly how many minutes to advance for rain vs. snow — fully customisable per condition.

📍

Location-Based Weather

Uses your current location or a location you specify to get accurate local weather data.

🔁

Recurring Alarms

Supports day-of-week repeat. Set your weekday alarm once and it runs every week automatically.

🔔

Shift Notification

When your alarm moves, you get a notification showing how many minutes it shifted and why.

No Login Required
